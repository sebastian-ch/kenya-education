Kenyan Primary School Enrollment in 2014

This map shows the enrollment numbers for Kenyan boys and girls in primary school. The slider at the bottom-left allows you to view data for each grade (1-8).

Education in Kenya

Primary education begins at the age of 5 to 7 after completing a year of kindergarden.
Free primary education was reintroduced in 2003, however, literacy rates are still low.
These levels decrease dramatically in more rural areas (see the Northeast on the map).
